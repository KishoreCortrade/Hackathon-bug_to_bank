# Problem Statement Brief
## AD Control Tower — Alter Domus Hackathon

---

## Customer

**Primary:** Operations Director, Alter Domus
**Secondary:** COO, Fund Accounting Manager, Compliance Manager, Investor Services Manager

---

## Problem

Alter Domus lacks a centralised operational intelligence capability. Operations leadership cannot assess the health of the fund portfolio in real time. Risk is discovered **after** client impact. Decision-making is delayed by manual data collection.

Leaders cannot easily answer:
- Which funds are at risk right now?
- Which teams are overloaded?
- Where are our operational bottlenecks?
- Which clients are likely to experience service issues?
- What do we do about it today?

---

## Current Process

1. Operations Managers collect status updates from team leads via email or meetings
2. Data is compiled manually into spreadsheets
3. Reports are produced weekly or monthly
4. Management reviews reports and identifies issues
5. Escalation and remediation begin — often **days or weeks** after the problem originated

**Result:** The organisation operates reactively. Issues are felt by clients before they are visible to management.

---

## Pain Points

| Pain Point | Business Impact |
|---|---|
| No single portfolio health view | Leaders spend 3–5 hours/week aggregating status across teams |
| Reactive issue discovery | SLA breaches and NAV delays found after client impact |
| No capacity visibility | Resource overloads invisible until quality degrades |
| No exception trending | Exception volumes rise unchecked between review cycles |
| Manual reporting | High effort, low frequency, always stale by the time it is read |
| Fragmented escalation | No consistent process — each team escalates differently |

---

## Business Case

| Risk Category | Estimated Impact |
|---|---|
| SLA breach per event | $200K – $2M AUM relationship at risk |
| Director reporting overhead | 25–30% of Director time on data collection vs. decisions |
| Staff turnover from overload | $50K – $75K per role in replacement cost |
| Client churn from late escalation | 15–25% increased churn probability per undetected escalation |

---

## Expected Outcome

A centralised platform — **AD Control Tower** — that provides:

- **Real-time Portfolio Health Score** (0–100 composite index)
- **Automated fund risk classification** (Critical / At Risk / Healthy)
- **Capacity analysis** with overload alerts
- **Bottleneck detection** at portfolio level
- **Prioritised recommended actions** (P1 / P2 / P3)
- **Executive insights** panel for COO-level decisions

This shifts Alter Domus operations from **reactive** to **predictive**.
